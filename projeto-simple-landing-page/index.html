What is fantasy ?

"Fantasy is a literary, cinematic, or musical genre where events are imaginary and generally have no relation to the real world. This genre is characterized by the presence of magical, supernatural, mythological elements, among others, which are accepted as normal within the context of the story. In literature, fantasy is one of most the popular genres and covers a wide variety of sub-genres, such as high fantasy (like "The Lord of the Rings"), urban fantasy (like "Harry Potter"), horror fantasy (like "Dracula") and many others. Fantasy allows authors to explore themes and ideas that may not be possible in other genres. It provides a way to comment on or criticize the real world through a distorted lens, and also provides an escape for readers, allowing them to explore worlds and situations that do in exist not reality. In addition, fantasy also refers to an idea with little basis in reality, often related to the desires and dreams of the individual. It can be an invention of the mind, a daydream, or an illusion."

Top 3 books

"The Fellowship of the Ring" is the first book in the "Lord of the Rings" trilogy by J.R.R. Tolkien. The story follows the hobbit Frodo Baggins and his companions on a journey to destroy the One Ring, a powerful relic that could allow the Dark Lord, Sauron, to dominate Middle-earth.

"Game of Thrones" is the first book in the "Song of Ice and Fire" series written by George R.R. Martin. The story takes place in a fictional medieval world where several noble families vie for control of the Iron Throne. The book is full of political intrigue, betrayal, violence, and elements of fantasy, such as dragons and mystical creatures. The plot revolves around the power struggle between the Stark, Lannister, and Targaryen houses, while supernatural threats begin to emerge beyond the great wall of ice.

"American Gods" is a book written by Neil Gaiman. The story follows Shadow Moon, a former convict who becomes embroiled in a conflict between ancient and new gods living in the United States. Shadow is hired by a mysterious man named Wednesday and embarks on a journey across the country, encountering gods from different mythologies who have adapted to American culture. The novel tackles themes such as immigration, faith, identity, and the influence of technology in contemporary society.

Top 3 strongest characters

Here are the most powerful characters in fantasy books

First

Gandalf - The "Lord of the Rings" trilogy by J.R.R. Tolkien introduces Gandalf, a powerful wizard known as the Grey. He is one of the Istari, sent to Middle-earth to help fight evil. Gandalf is a wise man and skilled in magic, able to cast powerful spells and manipulate the forces of nature. He plays a crucial role in the fight against Sauron, the Dark Lord, and is a valued leader and advisor to the Fellowship of the Ring.

Another Character

Harry Potter - The "Harry Potter" series by J.K. Rowling introduces Harry Potter, a young wizard destined to fight the evil Lord Voldemort. Harry is known for his courage and exceptional skill in magic, especially wand use. He has a special gift called "He-Who-Must-Not-Be-Named", which allows him to stand up to Voldemort. Over the course of the books, Harry becomes a leader in the fight against the forces of darkness and is seen as a symbol of hope for the wizarding community.

Third one

Vin - Brandon Sanderson's "Mistborn" series features Vin, a young thief with Allomancy skills, the art of burning metals to obtain special powers. Vin possesses the rare ability to burn herself into various metals, making her one of the most powerful people known as the Mistborn. She can manipulate the force of gravity, alter emotions, and become extremely agile and avid in combat. Vin becomes a key figure in the fight against the Final Empire, using his skills to challenge tyranny and protect the oppressed.
